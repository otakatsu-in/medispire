PAGE 4: FOR DOCTORS (/for-doctors)
Eligibility:

Step-by-Step Roadmap for Doctors (detailed, numbered):

Learn German (A1 → B2/C1) — Timeline: 12–18 months. Goethe Institut, online platforms, MediSpire's partnered language coaches.
Document Preparation — Degree certificates, transcripts, medical council registration, good standing certificate, work experience letters, passport, police clearance.
Get Documents Translated & Apostilled — Only certified translators accepted. MediSpire connects you with authorized translators.
Apply for Berufserlaubnis (Temporary License) — Issued by State Medical Chamber (Ärztekammer). Allows you to practice under supervision while pursuing Approbation.
Defizitbescheid (Deficiency Notice) — Ärztekammer assesses your qualification gaps. MediSpire helps you understand and respond to this document.
Fachsprachprüfung (FSP) — Medical Language Exam — B2/C1 level German proficiency specifically in medical context. MediSpire offers dedicated FSP preparation coaching.
Hospitation / Observership — 4–12 week observership in German hospitals. Builds network and improves German medical vocabulary. MediSpire assists with placements.
Job Application — CV in German format (Lebenslauf), cover letter (Motivationsschreiben), references. MediSpire reviews and prepares your complete application package.
Interview Preparation — Mock interviews, common German hospital HR questions, salary negotiation guidance.
Visa Application (Job Seeker / Work Visa) — Required documents: Berufserlaubnis, job offer, accommodation proof, health insurance. MediSpire provides visa checklist and support.
Approbation (Permanent Medical License) — Final full license issued after Gleichwertigkeitsprüfung (equivalence exam) or equivalence assessment. Long-term goal.
Arrive, Settle & Thrive — Accommodation, Anmeldung (registration), bank account, tax number, integration.
